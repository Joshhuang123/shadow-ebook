# 新概念英语青少版2A - 语法速查卡

## 一、现在进行时 (Present Progressive) - Unit 1-2

### 公式
```
主语 + am/is/are + 动词-ing + (其他)
```

### 用法
- 表示**此刻**正在进行的动作
- 口诀：**正在做某事，be 动词加 ing**

### 句型变化
| 句型 | 结构 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 肯定句 | I am working. | I'm reading a book. |
| 否定句 | I am not working. | I'm not watching TV. |
| 一般疑问句 | Am I working? | **Are** you **doing** something? |
| 特殊疑问句 | What am I doing? | **What are** they **doing**? |

### 动词-ing 变化规则
1. **直接加 -ing**: read → reading, play → playing
2. **去 e 加 -ing**: make → making, write → writing
3. **重读闭音节双写尾字母加 -ing**: sit → sitting, run → running

## 二、一般现在时 (Simple Present) - Unit 5-6, 10

### 公式
```
主语 + 动词(+s/es) + 其他    (描述习惯性动作)
```

### 用法
- 表示**经常性**或**习惯性**的动作
- 表示**客观事实**或**普遍真理**

### 第三人称单数变化 (he/she/it)
| 规则 | 动词例子 |
|------|---------|
| 一般加 -s | reads, plays, works |
| 字母s/x/ch/sh/o结尾加 -es | watches, goes, studies |
| 辅音字母+y → y+ies | study→studies, fly→flies |

### 时间标志词
- always, usually, often, sometimes, never
- every day/week/month/year
- in the morning/afternoon/evening

---

## 三、一般过去时 (Simple Past) - Unit 11-13

### 公式
```
主语 + 动词过去式 + 其他    (描述过去的动作)
```

### Be 动词过去式
| 现在 | 过去 |
|------|------|
| I am | I **was** |
| he/she/it is | he/she/it **was** |
| we/you/they are | we/you/they **were** |

### 规则动词过去式
| 规则 | 原形 → 过去式 |
|------|--------------|
| 一般加 -ed | work → worked, play → played |
| e 结尾加 -d | live → lived, hope → hoped |
| 辅音+y → y+ied | study → studied, cry → cried |
| 单元音重读闭音节结尾双写加 -ed | stop → stopped, plan → planned |

### 常见不规则动词 (必须背！)
| 原形 | 过去式 |
|------|--------|
| go | went |
| see | saw |
| eat | ate |
| have | had |
| take | took |
| come | came |
| get | got |
| buy | bought |
| read | read |

### 时间标志词
- yesterday (昨天)
- last night/week/month/year (上...)
- this morning/afternoon (今天早上/下午)
- in 2023 (在2023年)

## 四、频率表达 - Unit 7

| 频率 | 英语 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 100% | always | I always get up early. |
| 80% | usually | I usually have breakfast at 7. |
| 50% | often/sometimes | I often go to the cinema. |
| 20% | seldom | I seldom eat meat. |
| 0% | never | I never smoke. |

**提问**: How often do you exercise? → I exercise twice a week.

---

## 五、be going to 表计划 - Unit 8

### 公式
```
主语 + am/is/are + going to + 动词原形
```

### 例句
- What are you going to do? I'm going to visit my grandma.
- She is going to buy a new dress.
- They're going to have a party tomorrow.

---

## 六、want to do / want sb to do - Unit 9

### 公式
```
want to do = 想要做某事
want sb to do = 想要某人做某事
```

### 例句
- What do you want to do? I want to play football.
- I want you to help me with my English.

---

## 七、名词性物主代词 - Unit 3

| 形容词性物主代词 | 名词性物主代词 |
|-----------------|---------------|
| my | mine |
| your | yours |
| his | his |
| her | hers |
| our | ours |
| their | theirs |

**口诀**: 名词性物主代词 = 后面不能加名词，独立使用等于"my + 名词"

**例句**:
- This book is **mine**. (= my book)
- Is this your bag? No, it's **hers**. (= her bag)

---

## 八、祈使句 - Unit 4

### 公式
```
肯定句: 动词原形 + 其他
否定句: Don't + 动词原形 + 其他
```

### 例句
- **Open** the door, please.
- **Don't be** late!
- **Don't take** your gloves off.
- **Sit** down, please.

## 十、三时态对比速查

| 时态 | 用法 | 时间标志词 | 例句 |
|------|------|-----------|------|
| 现在进行时 | 此刻正在发生 | now, right now | I **am reading** now. |
| 一般现在时 | 习惯/经常发生 | always, usually, every day | I **read** books every day. |
| 一般过去时 | 过去发生的事 | yesterday, last week | I **read** a book yesterday. |
